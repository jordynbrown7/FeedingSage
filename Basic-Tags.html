Caring for Sage Lael

    Feeding Time Task List:
  • Pour 3 oz of milk in a 4 oz baby bottle
  • Using a Tommee Tippee warmer, heat up the milk
  • Test the milk temperature on your wrist
  • Get a burp cloth and feed Baby Sage (He guzzles so take frequent burp breaks)
